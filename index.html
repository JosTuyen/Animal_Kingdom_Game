ANIMAL KINGDOM

Rules:
1) The elephant will save endangered species by pushing them to origin place where the elephant appears at the beginning of the game

2) To move elephant you have to press :
w :up ; d :right ; s :down ; a :left ;
x :explode

3) One move or one turn costs 1 move
One explosion costs 100 moves

4) - Endangered species name and weight:
Penguin: 1 ; Zebra: 2 ; Giraffe: 3
- Other species name and weight:
Snake: 1 ; Gorilla: 2 ; Crocodile: 3

5) The max weight which you can push is 3
Press any key when you push the target to the origin to end
